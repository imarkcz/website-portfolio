my story

Welcome to my digital journey! It all began at the age of 18 when I discovered my passion for creativity through the lens of a camera. Capturing moments turned into a desire to share stories, leading me to embark on the creation of simple blog pages. As I delved into the world of web development, my curiosity deepened.

During my school days, I dipped my toes into HTML, laying the groundwork for what would become a fascination with crafting digital experiences. The more I learned, the more I hungered for knowledge. Last year marked a significant milestone as I invested in a CODECADEMY course for front-end development, unlocking the doors to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more.

Today, I am on a continuous learning journey, exploring the realms of web development, including frameworks, version control with Git, and collaborative coding on GitHub. Join me as I evolve from a curious teenager with a camera to a passionate learner and aspiring front-end developer, crafting digital experiences one code at a time.
